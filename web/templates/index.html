How the numbers are derived

Free Cash Flow (Current) uses Free Cash Flow (last 4 quarters) if Operating Cash Flow exceeds 1.5× Net Income, otherwise Net Income (latest fiscal year). Growth Yr 1–5 comes from analyst long-term estimates (falling back to historical EPS CAGR). Growth Yr 6–10 is the lesser of 15% or half the Yr 1–5 rate. Growth Yr 11–20 is long-term GDP growth + 1% (4.18% US, 7% China/HK). The discount rate is Risk Free Rate + Beta × Market Risk Premium.
