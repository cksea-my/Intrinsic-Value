What happens when you click Generate

01

Live data pull

Operating cash flow, free cash flow, net income, debt, cash, shares outstanding, beta and analyst growth estimates are fetched from Yahoo Finance.

02

Model rules applied

Free Cash Flow (Current) is chosen automatically (operating cash flow vs. 1.5× net income), growth rates are tiered across years 1–5, 6–10 and 11–20, and the discount rate is derived from beta.

03

Workbook download

Both the 20-year and 10-year tabs are populated in a copy of the template — every formula recalculates the moment you open it in Excel.
